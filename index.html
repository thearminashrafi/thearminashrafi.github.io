About

I am really passionate about open-ended questions and curious about the world.

My work mostly centers on reinforcement learning, currently focusing on representing behavioral knowledge and sample efficiency in grid-worlds. In general I enjoy open ended research into understanding learning agents in sequential decision making tasks.

Research interests: Continual RL, intrinsic motivation, Skill Acquisision, Knowledge Transfer, planning, causality, exploration, generalization.

Research

I mainly focus on reinforcement learning and questions of learning dynamics and transfer. I am also increasingly interested in lifelong learning, and novel methods like active inference.

  • Investigating the viability of latent search for policy represenations in Reinforcement Learning
  • Investigating the properties of Experience Replay, with goals to improve sample efficieny and learning dynamics.
  • General interest in Continual Learning and methods to achieve it as passion projects.
