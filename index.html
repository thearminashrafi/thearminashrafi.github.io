About

My work centers on neurosymbolic reinforcement learning, particularly program-synthesis agents (e.g., LEAPS VAE) and state representations for grid-world tasks. I enjoy designing clean JAX/Equinox systems and building tools that make iterative research faster and more reproducible.

Research interests: model-based RL, world models, planning, causal structure, program induction, generalization.
